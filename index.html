    ABOUT US

    In 2014, Raphaël established MOCA.

    MOCA draws inspiration from the Musee d'Orsay, a renowned art museum in France. It can also be interpreted as "Modern and Creative Art," reflecting our pursuit of innovation and uniqueness in macarons. Each macaron at MOCA is a creative masterpiece crafted by pastry chef Raphaël. By combining traditional and contemporary elements, MOCA presents a brand new pastry experience that showcases the fusion of flavors and aesthetics.

    Why choose Taiwan ?

    This island is home to numerous traditional delicacies and innovative flavors of pastries. We believe that MOCA's unique French macaron desserts will bring a whole new delight to pastry enthusiasts in Taiwan. On this island that blends Eastern and Western cultures, MOCA will showcase the essence of French pastry while incorporating local flavors and characteristics, creating a truly distinctive macaron experience.

    What does baking mean to you ?

    Baking holds deep significance for me. It serves as a means to express my creativity and unique style. The process of kneading dough and combining ingredients brings me a sense of calmness and relaxation. Baking is my passion for pursuing culinary excellence and a way to indulge in the pleasures of taste. It is an art form that has the power to touch hearts through flavors.
